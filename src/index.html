Why Hire Me?

I’ve been designing for small businesses & nonprofits for 20 years, so I’ve seen how much you’re juggling: the never-ending to-do list, the limited hours, the constant pull between what needs to get done and what you wish you had time to focus on. My goal is to give you some of that time back by making design feel less like a chore and more like a quiet relief.

I don’t only care about how things look (though, of course, I care about that too!); I care about how they work, whether that’s a donation form that actually functions or a layout that loads properly on mobile.

My goal is to make your day-to-day just a little bit easier. Whether I'm fixing a website emergency or creating a flyer, I'll show up with clarity, thoughtfulness, and zero unnecessary stress!
