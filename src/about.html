Hi, I’m Toni

I’m a graphic and web designer for kind humans doing good work.

I help small businesses and nonprofits bring their ideas to life through thoughtful design - whether it’s a website that actually works (imagine that!), print materials that feel polished and intentional, or social media images that make your work look as good as it is.

What I Value

  • Intentional simplicity. Designs that do their job unnecessary complications just make sense.
  • Efficiency + ease. Design should make your life easier, not more complicated.
  • Consistency. Your brand deserves to feel like home everywhere it appears, from business cards to websites.
  • Accessibility. I prioritize layouts, colors, and content that are usable by everyone.
  • Room to Breathe. Hierarchy and whitespace are non-negotiables (they’re like a comfy blanket for your eyes).
  • Design with purpose. Everything I create is rooted in what you need to communicate, not what trends dictate.

My Story

I’ve been a designer in some form or another since way back in the 1900s (as my teenager lovingly calls it). I taught myself to code in middle school, fell in love with graphic design working on my high school yearbook, and I haven’t stopped since.

After graduating from Florida State University (go Noles!), I worked with state agencies, large corporations, small businesses, and nonprofits - but it’s the last two that feel like home. I have so much respect for folks doing the brave thing: building businesses, supporting their communities, and showing up even when it’s hard.

These days, I can be found running Tesori Design from my cozy home office in Colorado...usually rocking Eras Tour merch, with my planner (a necessity!) and a penguin mascot named Ty* keeping me company.
*Long story, but he’s part personal history, part branding spirit guide.

I'm a good fit for folks who...

  • Care deeply about their work and want it to look as thoughtful as it is.
  • Are DIY-ing out of necessity - or putting things off because it feels too overwhelming.
  • Haven’t touched their website in three years and honestly are afraid to look.
  • Want someone to just handle it so they can get back to doing their actual work.
  • Could use a calm, capable human on their team.
  • Want to feel more confident sharing their brand with the world.
