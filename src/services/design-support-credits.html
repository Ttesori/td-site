Design Credits

A flexible way to get ongoing design help.

Think of design credits like a flexible design gift card. You buy a bundle of hours now, and use them whenever you need help — whether it’s a new flyer, a quick site update, or a cleanup of something you’ve been meaning to fix for ages.

Sometimes a little help goes a long way.

You don’t have to commit to ongoing support to get something polished and on-brand.

How Design Credits Work

You purchase a set number of design hours up front, and I track them as you use them. Credits are good for a full year and can be used for any type of design or website work; whether you need a flyer, a few social posts, or a slide deck. It’s a simple, low-pressure way to get the design help you need.

How To Use Your Credits

  1. You send a request whenever something comes up. No need to wait; just message me when you’re ready.
  2. I’ll give you a time estimate and a start date. That way you know exactly how many hours will be used before I begin.
  3. You give the go-ahead. Once you’re ready, I’ll add you to my schedule and get to work.
  4. I track your hours and keep you updated. You’ll always know how much time you’ve used and what’s left.
