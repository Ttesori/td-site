What’s Included

Here’s what I’ll handle during your Website Refresh, so you can stop worrying about your website and get back to what matters.

  • Layout & Style Refresh. Update your site’s layout, colors, fonts, and overall style so it looks current, clean, and aligned with your brand.

  • Content Updates. We'll update page text, images, and navigation, so your visitors can easily find the most current information.

  • Broken Links & SEO Tune-up. Fix broken links and improve key SEO elements (titles, descriptions, headings) to help your refreshed site perform better in search results.

  • Primary Action Testing & Fixes. Test and troubleshoot key visitor actions like contact forms, donation buttons, and sign-up forms so they work smoothly and send you the right information.

  • Mobile & Accessibility Improvements. Tidy your site so it works well on mobile devices and is easier to navigate for everyone.

  • Plugin & Theme Updates. If you're on Wordpress, I'll safely update your plugins and theme, and troubleshoot common display or functionality issues.

  • 1 Month of Support After Launch. I’ll be available for tweaks, adjustments, or questions for 1 month after your refresh is live, so you’re not left hanging.

  • Pricing at a Glance
