How Website SOS Works For You

Website SOS sessions are done-for-you website fixes — I take nagging website tasks off your plate, so you can spend more time on what you love.

  • Content Updates. Need to update text, swap images, or add new information? I’ll handle your content updates so your site stays current and your visitors can find what they need.

  • Layout & Style Fixes. Weird spacing? Text overlapping? Things just not looking right? I’ll troubleshoot and tidy up your site’s layout so it works (and looks good) on any device.

  • Mobile Display Issues. If your site looks fine on desktop but is wonky or broken on phones or tablets, I’ll adjust it to be mobile-friendly and easy to navigate on any size device.

  • Broken Links & Navigation. I can find and fix broken links, update your menus, and make sure visitors can get where they need to go — no dead ends, no frustration.

  • Contact Forms & Calls-to-Action. If your contact form isn’t working, I’ll test it and troubleshoot the issue. I can also update buttons and calls-to-action so they’re easy for visitors to use.

  • Plugin & Theme Updates. Outdated plugins or themes can cause glitches or security risks. I can update them safely and resolve common issues that come up afterward.
